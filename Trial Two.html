2.Trial of Courage – Aeacus Gatekeeper
(Keeper of the keys to Hades)
Only the brave may cross. Fear is not the enemy—denial is.

Test: The soul must choose how they faced their greatest fear in life.









Purpose: This trial judges the core essence of a soul's purpose, the fire (or void) behind every step.
