1.Who rules the Underworld?
✅ c) Hades

2.Which river must souls cross to enter the Underworld?
✅ a) River Styx (Though technically, they cross the Acheron first, but Styx is the most famous!)

3.What does the River Lethe do to those who drink from it?
✅ b) Makes them forget everything

4.What three-headed creature guards the gates of the Underworld?
✅ b) Cerberus

5.Where do the truly wicked souls end up?
✅ c) Tartarus

6.Who ferries souls across the river into the Underworld?
✅ b) Charon

7.Which hero once entered the Underworld and returned?
✅ a) Hercules (Though Theseus and Odysseus also went, Hercules is the most famous for his Twelve Labors!)

8.Who is the goddess of spring and also the Queen of the Underworld?
✅ b) Persephone (She was kidnapped by Hades but later became his queen.)

9.What do you need to pay Charon for a ride across the river?
✅ a) A golden drachma (Without payment, souls are stuck at the river's edge forever.)

10.Which Underworld judge was once a mortal king?
✅ a) Minos (One of the three judges, along with Rhadamanthus and Aeacus!)
